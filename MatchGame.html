Match Game

The game mechanics are simple. Click on an image to reveal it. It will stay revealed until you click on a second image. If both images match, they will stay revealed. If they don’t, they will both change back into the hidden icon. Once all of the images have been revealed the game ends. The score is determined by the number of clicks. Each time you click on one of the images, you score will increment by 1, where the lowest possible score is 16 if you already know where all of the images are or you are insanely lucky). When the reset button is clicked, all of the images will return to the hidden icon and the score will be reset to 0.
